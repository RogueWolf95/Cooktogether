Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 12 chicken wings
- 2 tablespoons ghost pepper hot sauce
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a small bowl, mix together the ghost pepper hot sauce, melted butter, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Place the chicken wings on the prepared baking sheet and brush the sauce mixture over them.
4. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until they are cooked through and crispy.
5. Optional: To make them extra hot, brush another layer of the sauce mixture over the wings halfway through cooking.
6. Remove the wings from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Devilishly Hot Ghost Pepper Wings are spicy and flavorful chicken wings coated in a fiery ghost pepper sauce. These wings are perfect for spice lovers who crave a fiery kick.
