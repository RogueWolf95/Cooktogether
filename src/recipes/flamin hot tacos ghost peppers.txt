Part 1: Ingredients for 2 servings
- 6 small soft taco shells
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
- 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/4 cup chopped onions
- 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
- 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 ghost peppers (optional, for extra heat)
- Salt and pepper to taste

Part 2: Concise Instructions
1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Warm the soft taco shells in a microwave or oven.
3. Assemble the tacos by placing a scoop of ground beef onto each taco shell.
4. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped onions, sliced jalapenos, and shredded cheddar cheese.
5. If desired, thinly slice the ghost peppers and sprinkle them over the tacos for extra heat.
6. Serve the flamin hot tacos immediately.

Part 3: Short Description of dish
Flamin hot tacos are a spicy twist on traditional tacos, filled with seasoned ground beef and topped with a mix of fresh vegetables. For the extra daring, adding ghost peppers takes the heat to another level.
