Part 1: List the Ingredients for 2 servings

- 1 cup crushed graham crackers
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
- Ground cinnamon (optional, for garnish)

Part 2: Write concise instructions

1. In a bowl, mix together crushed graham crackers and melted butter until well combined.
2. Press the graham cracker mixture into the bottom of a muffin tin, dividing it evenly between 2 cups. Press down firmly to create a crust.
3. In another bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add pumpkin puree, granulated sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
4. Spoon the pumpkin cheesecake mixture evenly over the graham cracker crust in the muffin tin cups.
5. Place the muffin tin in the refrigerator and chill for at least 2 hours to set.
6. Once set, remove the pumpkin cheesecake bites from the muffin tin by gently running a knife around the edges and lifting them out.
7. Optional: Top with a dollop of whipped cream and sprinkle with ground cinnamon for garnish.
8. Serve and enjoy!

Part 3: Short Description of dish
These pumpkin cheesecake bites are a delicious and easy-to-make dessert that combines the creamy richness of cheesecake with the warm flavors of pumpkin pie. Perfect for fall or any time you're craving a sweet and satisfying treat!
