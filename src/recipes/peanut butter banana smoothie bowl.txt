Part 1: Ingredients for 2 servings:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 4 tablespoons of peanut butter
- 1 cup of almond milk
- 1 tablespoon of honey
- 1/2 cup of rolled oats
- 2 tablespoons of chia seeds
- Toppings of your choice (such as sliced bananas, shredded coconut, or granola)

Part 2: Instructions:
1. In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, peanut butter, almond milk, honey, rolled oats, and chia seeds.
2. Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and creamy.
3. Pour the smoothie mixture into two bowls.
4. Add your desired toppings, such as sliced bananas, shredded coconut, or granola.
5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Part 3: Description of the final dish:
The peanut butter banana smoothie bowl is a creamy and delicious breakfast or snack option. It combines the flavors of ripe bananas and peanut butter, along with the added crunch and texture from the toppings. It's a perfect combination of healthy ingredients that will keep you full and satisfied.
