Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 1/2 cup chocolate pudding mix
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup crushed chocolate sandwich cookies
- 2 gummy worms

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. In a small bowl, combine the chocolate pudding mix and milk. Whisk until well blended.
2. Divide the crushed chocolate sandwich cookies evenly between 2 serving cups.
3. Pour the prepared chocolate pudding over the crushed cookies, dividing it equally between the cups.
4. Place a gummy worm on top of each pudding cup.
5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the pudding to set.
6. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Dirt Pudding Cups are a fun and indulgent dessert, featuring layers of creamy chocolate pudding and crushed cookies, topped with a gummy worm. They are reminiscent of an edible mini garden, perfect for kids and those young at heart.
