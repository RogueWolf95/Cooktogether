Part 1: Ingredients for 2 servings of Ghost Pepper Chili:

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
- 2 ghost peppers, diced (remove seeds for less heat)
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped cilantro

Part 2: Instructions:

1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess grease.

2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté for about 5 minutes until the onion is translucent.

3. Stir in the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and diced ghost peppers. Mix well.

4. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir until all the ingredients are combined.

5. Bring the chili to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

6. Taste and adjust the seasonings if needed. If you prefer a spicier chili, you can add more diced ghost peppers.

7. Serve the ghost pepper chili hot, topped with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped cilantro if desired.

Part 3: Description of the final dish:
Ghost Pepper Chili is a fiery and flavorful dish, perfect for those who enjoy a spicy kick in their chili. The combination of ground beef, kidney beans, and diced ghost peppers creates a rich and hearty chili that will warm you up from the inside out.
