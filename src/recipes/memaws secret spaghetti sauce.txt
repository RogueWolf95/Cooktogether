Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14.5 ounces) crushed tomatoes
- 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Once cooked, remove any excess fat.
2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, ensuring it is well combined with the meat and onion mixture.
4. Season the sauce with dried basil, dried oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
5. Allow the sauce to simmer over low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Memaw's secret spaghetti sauce is a hearty and flavorful pasta sauce made with ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. It is a perfect accompaniment to spaghetti, bringing extra depth and richness to any pasta dish.
