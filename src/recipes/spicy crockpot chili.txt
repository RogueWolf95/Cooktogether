Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 5
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
- 2 cups beef broth
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to desired spice level)
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
2. Add diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the ground beef. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the onion is translucent.
3. Transfer the cooked ground beef mixture to a slow cooker.
4. Add kidney beans, black beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper to the slow cooker. Stir well to combine.
5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours, or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until flavors are well-combined and the chili has thickened.
6. Serve hot and garnish with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, diced jalapenos, or chopped cilantro.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Spicy crockpot chili is a hearty and flavorful dish made with ground beef, beans, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. It is cooked slowly in a crockpot to allow the flavors to meld together, resulting in a satisfying chili with a kick of spice.
