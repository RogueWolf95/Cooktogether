Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 1 cup of crushed graham crackers
- 1/2 cup of melted butter
- 1 cup of pumpkin puree
- 1/2 cup of brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon of ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon of cloves
- 1/2 cup of caramel sauce
- 1/4 cup of cannabis-infused butter (optional)
- 1/2 cup of chopped pecans

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers and melted butter, mixing until well combined.
3. Press the graham cracker mixture into the bottom of a greased 8x8 inch baking dish to form the crust.
4. In a separate bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, caramel sauce, and cannabis-infused butter (if desired) until smooth.
5. Pour the pumpkin mixture over the prepared crust, spreading it evenly.
6. Sprinkle the chopped pecans over the top of the pumpkin mixture.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
8. Remove from the oven and let cool completely before cutting into bars.
9. Serve and enjoy!

Part 3: Description
Pumpkin Patch Caramel Cannabis Bars are delicious and indulgent treats that combine the flavors of pumpkin, caramel, and warm spices. The graham cracker crust provides a crunchy base, while the creamy and spiced pumpkin layer adds depth of flavor. Topped with chopped pecans for a nutty and crunchy texture, these bars are the perfect fall dessert or snack.
