Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 4 flour tortillas
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 ghost pepper, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/4 cup diced red onion
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. In a skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
2. Add the ground beef and ghost pepper to the skillet and cook until the beef is browned and cooked through.
3. Season the beef with salt and pepper to taste.
4. In a separate bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice.
5. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.
6. Assemble the tacos by placing a generous portion of the ground beef mixture onto each tortilla.
7. Top with the tomato and onion mixture.
8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Flamin Hot Ghost Pepper Tacos are a spicy and flavorful twist on traditional tacos. The combination of the fiery ghost pepper with the savory ground beef and fresh toppings creates a mouthwatering and unforgettable meal.
