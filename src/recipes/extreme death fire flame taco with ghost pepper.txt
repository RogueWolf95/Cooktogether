Part 1: Ingredients for 2 servings
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ghost pepper, finely chopped
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 4 small tortillas
- Shredded lettuce
- Diced tomatoes
- Shredded cheese
- Sour cream (optional)
- Sliced jalapenos (optional)
- Hot sauce (optional)

Part 2: Instructions
1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up into crumbles as it cooks.
2. Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and ghost pepper to the skillet. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic becomes fragrant.
3. Stir in the cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
4. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.
5. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the spicy beef mixture onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, sliced jalapenos, and hot sauce, if desired.
6. Fold the tortillas in half and serve immediately.

Part 3: Description
The Extreme Death Fire Flame Taco with Ghost Pepper is a fiery and flavorful taco that combines the heat of ghost pepper with savory spices. The spicy beef filling is balanced by cool and refreshing toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese. It's a perfect dish for those who love intense heat and bold flavors.
