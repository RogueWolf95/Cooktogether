Part 1: List of Ingredients for 2 servings
- 2 slices of sourdough bread
- 2 frozen beef patties
- 4 slices of mozzarella cheese
- 1 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1 teaspoon of onion powder
- 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
- 1 can of tomato soup

Part 2: Step-by-step Instructions
1. Preheat your oven to the temperature indicated on the frozen beef patty packaging.
2. While the oven is preheating, lightly toast the sourdough bread slices.
3. In a small bowl, mix together the black pepper, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder. Set aside.
4. Once the oven is ready, place the frozen beef patties on a baking sheet and bake according to the package instructions.
5. While the beef patties are cooking, pour the tomato soup into a small saucepan and heat it on medium-low heat until warm. Stir occasionally.
6. Once the beef patties are cooked through, place two slices of mozzarella cheese on top of each patty.
7. Return the patties to the oven for a couple of minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
8. Remove the toasted sourdough bread from the toaster and place each beef patty with melted cheese on a slice.
9. Sprinkle the spice mixture from step 3 over the melted cheese.
10. Pour the warm tomato soup into small bowls to serve alongside the sandwiches.

Part 3: Short Description of Dish
This recipe creates delicious sourdough beef melt sandwiches with melted mozzarella cheese on top of juicy beef patties. Served with a side of tomato soup for a comforting and satisfying meal.
