Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 2 cups of chicken broth
- 1/4 cup of finely diced onion
- 1/4 cup of finely diced carrot
- 1/4 cup of finely diced celery
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon of dried thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. In a large saucepan, combine the chicken broth, onion, carrot, celery, garlic, thyme, and bay leaf.
2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
3. After simmering, strain the consommé through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the solids. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Serve the consommé hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Consommé is a clear and flavorful soup made by simmering a flavorful broth and then clarifying it. It is a light and delicate dish often served as a starter or palate cleanser before a main course.

Part 4: Provide an integer value between one and ten to indicate the spice factor
0 (Consommé is traditionally not spicy)
