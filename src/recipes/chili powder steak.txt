Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 2 steaks (preferably ribeye or sirloin)
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat.
2. In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
3. Rub both sides of each steak with the spice mixture, ensuring an even coating.
4. Drizzle the vegetable oil over the steaks and massage it into the spices.
5. Place the steaks on the preheated grill or pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare doneness, or adjust the cooking time according to your preference.
6. Remove the steaks from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
7. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve it hot.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Chili powder steak is a flavorful and spicy dish made by rubbing steaks with a blend of chili powder, salt, and black pepper before grilling or pan-searing them. The result is a juicy and tender steak with a delicious kick of heat.
