Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 1 cucumber
- 2 cups cubed watermelon
- 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp honey (optional)
- A pinch of salt

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. Start by peeling the cucumber, then slice it in half lengthwise and remove the seeds. Cut the cucumber into thin, half-moon slices.
2. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber slices, cubed watermelon, and fresh mint leaves.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, honey (if using), and a pinch of salt. Drizzle the dressing over the salad ingredients.
4. Gently toss the salad until all the ingredients are well coated with the dressing.
5. Transfer the salad to serving plates and garnish with additional mint leaves, if desired.

Part 3: Description of the final dish
The Refreshing Cucumber Watermelon Salad is a light and refreshing summer dish that combines the crispness of cucumber with the sweet juiciness of watermelon. The addition of fresh mint and a tangy lime dressing enhances the flavors and makes it a perfect side dish or light meal option.
