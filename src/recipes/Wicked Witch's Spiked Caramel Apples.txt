Part 1: List the Ingredients for a serving count of 2
- 2 large apples
- 1 cup caramel candies
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons spiced rum
- 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)
- popsicle sticks or skewers

Part 2: Write the concise Instructions
1. Wash and dry the apples. Insert the popsicle sticks or skewers into the tops of the apples, making sure they are secure.
2. In a medium saucepan, melt the caramel candies, heavy cream, and butter over medium heat, stirring continuously until smooth.
3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the spiced rum.
4. Dip each apple into the caramel mixture, using a spoon to help coat the apple evenly.
5. Optional step: Roll the caramel-coated apples in chopped nuts for added crunch.
6. Place the caramel apples on a parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate for about 30 minutes to allow the caramel to set.
7. Remove from the refrigerator and serve.

Part 3: Provide a short Description of the final dish
Wicked Witch's Spiked Caramel Apples are deliciously sweet and boozy treats perfect for Halloween or any spooky occasion. The crisp apples are coated in a rich and creamy caramel infused with spiced rum, resulting in a wickedly indulgent dessert.
